Click on map to set your start point. A green flag will be shown marking it. Hide this help clicking on the help icon at left.
Your start point is set, now, click on green flag to choose the direction that you will walk. You may change your start point by clicking on map again or dragging the green flag.
Choose your direction sliding the aim or typing the number on the properly field. The compass shows the direction on map. After this, click on 'Walk' to draw your path.
Your path is drawn! Now you can see what is near by your path by clicking on it. An eye icon will be displayed.
Your point of view is set. Now, click on eye icon to choose the range of your view. You may change your point of view by clicking on map again or dragging the eye icon.
Choose te range of your point of view on the selection field. After this, click on 'View' to see the near by places around the eye icon.
If exists points near by the eye icon, they are shown on map and on the frame at right. You can click on house icons to see information about this near by point. Use the Reset button above to draw another path.
Your start point is set, now, click on map again to set where you want to pass. You may change your start point by dragging the green flag.
The start point and the point where you will pass are set. Now, click on green or red flag to draw the path that you are going to walk. You may change the position of both flags by dragging or clicking on map.
With 2 points you have a line and a direction. Click on 'Walk' to draw your path.
